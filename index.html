EST 2019

Designed by Morson Group Architects, St John XXIII College Performance Hub is an ambitious multi-purpose centre dedicated to an environment for those students who want to thrive in theatre, dance and music.

A uniquely distinct triangular shape defines the performance hub with its bold yet functional, acoustic design for the hall. The space’s sweeping angular ceilings and other design features lent themselves to subtle lighting challenges. The appointment of JHA Consulting Engineers brought a collaborative team onboard to resolve the Hall’s lighting with precision.
